You are a veterinarian, physio, osteopath or do research in equestrian sports

When it comes to understanding the system: rider-saddle-horse, you cannot avoid measuring the pressure.
There is hardly any other area where the following applies: “It turns out differently than you think”.
Predicted hoped-for effects must be substantiated. Taking into account almost infinite factors such as horse anatomy, material properties, rider influencing factors and much much more...

They teach? Help your students!

Riders are often unaware of how messages about the seat can be conveyed to the horse.
Explaining or understanding "riding over the seat" is very difficult.
Especially people who learn better visually, it helps to visualize their own influence... The cause-effect principle represented by the saddle tester... Convince yourself!

Do you sell saddles or are you a saddler?

Prove your saddle fit! The rider-saddle-horse system is highly complex and subject to constant change. A saddle that fits today will no longer have to in 6 months. Protect yourself against recourse and explain to your customer why a saddle that was suitable at the time is now unsuitable. This is only possible with electronic measurement data!
